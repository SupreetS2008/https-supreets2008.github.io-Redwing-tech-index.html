BUILDING DEPARTMENT

Building with passion

Assembly

Robot Assembly Overview

Our team followed a structured mechanical workflow, beginning with frame construction, drivetrain installation, and subsystem alignment. Students emphasized bolt-consistent patterns, redundancy checks, and quality assurance throughout each phase.

Prototyping

Iteration & Controlled Testing

Early prototypes included claw revisions, arm rotation mechanisms, and intake configurations. Students produced multiple controlled tests recorded with detailed notes and failure-mode assessments.

Manufacturing Steps

Fabrication & Component Prep

Students used cutting tools, drills, and 3D printed components to fabricate parts, ensuring tolerances met CAD specifications. All components were labeled and documented before final installation.

Testing Cycles

Trial Runs & Adjustments

Testing cycles included drive motion trials, subsystem stress checks, and repeated autonomous validation. Students logged outcomes and implemented incremental adjustments to improve performance consistency.

Final Integration

Completion & System Alignment

After all components were validated, the team assembled the robot into a finalized configuration. Final checks ensured alignment of electronics, secure connections, and functional compliance with BEST rules.
